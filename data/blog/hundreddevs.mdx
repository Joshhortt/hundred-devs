**Author & Mentor:** [Leon Noel](https://leonnoel.com/100devs/)

This article is dedicated to making it straightforward for the catch-up crew and newcomers to locate the follow-along materials, the homework, and the class files instead of sifting through all of the more recent Discord messages.
